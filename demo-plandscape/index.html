PLATFORM · IN ONE PARAGRAPH

SymbiOS™ is a computational design platform for ecological intelligence — a hybrid of ArcGIS, BIM, and real-time climate sensing built for landscape architects, urban ecologists, and planners. As a designer, it's the climate adaptation + computation software we wish we had, so we're building it ourselves.

VANA is Module 01: a field-deployable plant intelligence database for climate-vulnerable coastal contexts across SE Asia, the Middle East, and the South Pacific — ground zero for the climate crisis. Species data, ecological precedent, and climate adaptation scoring. At the point of need.

Plants are fractal beings; infinite at all scales. What does it sound like to listen to their leaves — to their wind-song — to finally begin to understand them? VA·NA is the nexus of computational design and living landscape. Roots and recursion, leaves and lexicons — merging procedurally, parametrically. We extract the generative grammar of living systems and expose it as a computational palette.
