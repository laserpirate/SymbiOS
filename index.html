SymbiOS Mesh

Eco-sensing + GIS + predictive digital twins that give coastal cities a live picture of what the water is doing now—and what it will do next.

What we’re building

SymbiOS turns shorelines into living infrastructure: a mesh of low-cost sensors, edge AI, and landscape-scale models that continuously map flood, surge, and erosion risk.

The platform is designed for planners, insurers, and communities who need actionable resilience intelligence—not just static PDFs.

  • Real-time coastal risk scorecards
  • Neighborhood-scale flood and surge scenarios
  • APIs for insurers, cities, and climate-tech partners
